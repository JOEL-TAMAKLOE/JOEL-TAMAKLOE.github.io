Kira Nappies is a **new baby care brand** entering the market with a focus on comfort, reliability, and peace of mind for parents. The challenge was to **develop a distinctive visual identity and packaging system** that makes the products stand out while establishing trust and approachability for a new audience.

The project involved **strategic brand ideation and visual mapping**, creating a cohesive color palette, typography, and graphic elements that communicate softness, care, and clarity. Each product was designed to be instantly recognizable while maintaining a consistent and unified brand appearance across the range.

Through **visual system optimization and packaging refinements**, the designs achieve both functional clarity and emotional appeal. Layouts, imagery, and supporting graphics work together to create a strong shelf presence, giving Kira Nappies a professional yet approachable identity that resonates with parents and caregivers.

Client: Kira Nappies (new baby care brand)
Services: Brand identity, packaging design, visual system development, brand positioning

Yobysin is a baby care brand associated with a range of hygiene and infant care products such as disposable wipes and diapers manufactured within a global supply ecosystem for non‑woven personal and baby care essentials. The visual identity and packaging system I developed for Yobysin was conceived to elevate everyday caregiving essentials into a cohesive brand experience that parents can instantly recognize and trust.

The design process involved deep **ideation and concept refinement**, establishing a visual language that balances approachability with functional clarity. I defined a **visual hierarchy** for packaging that improves product communication at a glance, using color, iconography, and layout to differentiate product types while maintaining a unified family style. The color palette was selected to convey gentle care and reliability, with graphic elements that enhance both emotional appeal and usability.

Through **prototyping and narrative design**, each package was crafted to support intuitive interaction, making the Yobysin range feel cohesive on shelf and reassuring in the hands of caregivers. The result is a brand identity that strengthens recognition, promotes trust, and aligns the visuals with the brand’s core promise of dependable baby care essentials.

Client: Yobysin baby care products
Services: Brand identity, packaging design, visual system development, narrative design
